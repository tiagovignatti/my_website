During my recent stint around [Portugal](https://pt.wikipedia.org/wiki/Festa_de_S%C3%A3o_Jo%C3%A3o_do_Porto) and [Spain](https://webengineshackfest.org/), I shared my brother's book "A Máquina da Natureza" with lots of people.

Whenever I mentioned that the book **is not just a typical computer science book**, but one that
introduces computer science in an unusual way — not as a branch of engineering, but as a discipline that reaches into philosophy and the natural sciences, exploring the connection with human consciousness and thought, the reaction was always the same: people got, understandably, wild about it!!

Ok, but what actually "exploring the connection with human consciousness and thought" even mean? That's it, you got to check it 😀 But at the moment the book has only been published in Portuguese. That's why I decided to put together a page about it in English to help spread the word. Go there now: [www.vignatti.com/andre/](/andre/)
